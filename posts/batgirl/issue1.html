Batgirl

ISSUE ONE

April 8, 2020 by mysterious_stranger

SINS OF THE MOTHER: FEARFUL SYMMETRY

PAGE ONE

Full-page splash.

PANEL ONE

Wide exterior shot of a MOUNTAIN CLIFF. The terrain looks pretty rugged, with snow dotting parts of the ground and rocky soil surrounding it. Though the remains of an ANCIENT TEMPLE can be seen carved into the cliffs in the background, it looks like no one’s been here in a very long time. No one, that is, except for the dozen or so PEOPLE gathered by the edge of the cliff. Our shot is too distant to get good details, but they appear to be engaged in some form of BATTLE, with one lone figure in the center fending off attacks.

NARRATION CAPTION
Mongolia. The Altai Mountains. 4000 meters.

PAGE TWO

Nine-panel grid.

PANEL ONE

Medium action shot of BEN TURNER (BRONZE TIGER), who’s wearing a climate-appropriate dark orange coat and a dark orange mask over the lower half of his face with the outlines of tiger fangs drawn on them, is in the thick of it, alongside a number of ASSASSINS. The assassins are nondescript, all dressed in cold weather outfits and full-face masks. They look distinctly ninja-y. Ben is doing pretty well, punching one of them across the face, but he’s clearly taken some damage, too.

DOT (VO) *
Begin file: Benjamin Turner. Aliases: Bronze Tiger, Benjamin Stanley. Known affiliates: G.O.O.D., Task Force X, Richard Dragon, Lady Shiva.

CAPTION
* To see Dot's deal, read the Batgirl story in the DCW Alpha Oneshot!

PANEL TWO

Medium action shot. Ben springs up into a side kick, totally horizontal, one of his hands resting on the shoulder of the assassin behind him as his foot connects solidly with another assassin’s face.

DOT (VO)
Relevant information: Benjamin Turner is one of the most talented martial artists in the world.

PANEL THREE

Close-up on Ben’s face. His mouth is hidden by his mask, but his eyes are hard, focused, as he blocks a kick from one of the assassins.

DOT (VO)
Turner is one of the three most well-known students of the renowned O-Sensei, alongside his goddaughter, Sandra “Shiva” Wu-san, and the reclusive Richard Dragon.

PANEL FOUR

Medium action shot. Ben’s been seized about either wrist by the assassins, struggling against their grip as they hold his arms out to either side.

DOT (VO)
However, most of Turner’s reputation was earned after he was kidnapped and brainwashed by the League of Assassins, becoming the killer known as Bronze Tiger.

PANEL FIVE

Close-up as Ben, still being held in place, takes a PUNCH TO THE FACE from one of the other assassins.

DOT (VO)
After his rescue and deprogramming, Turner was recruited to serve on Amanda Waller’s Task Force X, for which he has frequently served as field commander over the years.

PANEL SIX

Zoom out slightly. We can see that Ben is dangerously near the edge of the cliff… and that the assassins are dragging him closer yet. He struggles, but he doesn’t seem to be winning.

DOT (VO)
Recently, however, Turner appears to have departed for a personal mission. Details of his goal are currently unknown.

PANEL SEVEN

Close-up of Ben’s BOOTS, digging into and pushing against the ground. The edge of the cliff is crumbling beneath his boots.

PANEL EIGHT

Close-up on Ben’s eyes. He looks alarmed.

PANEL NINE

Wide shot, and we can see that Ben is now falling off of the cliff. It’s pretty high up.

DOT (VO)
Last known location: Ulgii, Mongolia, 3 days ago.

PAGE THREE

Nine-panel grid.

PANEL ONE

Close-up. Ben is in free fall next to the cliff face.

BEN
Shit. Shit. Shit!

PANEL TWO

Medium shot. Ben, still falling, fumbles in his coat for something.

PANEL THREE

Close-up as Ben digs a pair of METAL CLAWS into the rock face of the cliff.

PANEL FOUR

Medium shot from below. Ben hangs there for a moment, exhaling heavily (SFX: Whew!) We can see the edge of the cliff above him.

PANEL FIVE

Same shot as Ben looks up, surprised and confused. COMBAT SOUNDS now come from the cliff above him.

BEN
The hell?

PANEL SIX

Ben, still hanging there, startles as an ASSASSIN tied to a THICK METAL CORD falls over the cliff next to him, barely missing him. (SFX: WHMP)

PANEL SEVEN

Ben stares at the dangling assassin next to him.

PANEL EIGHT

Ben starts climbing the cord.

PANEL NINE

Close-up. A GLOVED HAND closes around Ben’s wrist as he reaches the top. He looks up, somewhere between surprised and wary.

BEN
… Shiva? That you?

PAGE FOUR

Two panels, one splash and one inset in the lower right corner.

PANEL ONE

Splash panel. From Ben’s POV, we see the kneeling figure of a woman in her late teens. Black gloves. Black boots. Black cape. Black bodysuit, with a yellow outline of a Bat stretched across it. But rather than a mask, we see the bare face of CASSANDRA CAIN, smiling down at Ben.

CASS
No. I’m not.

PANEL TWO

Close-up of Ben’s shocked reaction.

PAGE FIVE

Nine-panel grid.

PANEL ONE

Medium wide shot. Cass and Ben are now both standing beside the cliff, amidst the group of unconscious assassins. Ben is brushing himself off, while Cass appears to be pulling the assassin from the previous page back up.

CASS
You okay?

BEN
All fine, except for my pride.

PANEL TWO

Close-up of Ben as he surveys the unconscious assassins.

BEN
Was a time when I could’ve handled all these guys without breaking a sweat. Guess it’s rough getting old.

PANEL THREE

Close-up of Cass as she finishes hoisting the unconscious assassin back up, setting him down gently. Her expression is neutral, even vaguely pleasant.

CASS
They were good. You… surprised?

BEN (OFFSCREEN)
That they were good?

CASS
No. They… surprise you?

BEN (OFFSCREEN)
Oh. Yeah.

PANEL FOUR

Medium shot. Ben stares at Cass, arms folded. Scrutinizing her.

BEN
You’re her, aren’t you? Cain and Shiva’s kid.

CASS
Yes.

PANEL FIVE

Full-body shot of Cass as she stands, brushing her hands against each other.

BEN (OFFSCREEN)
God. You look so much like her. Your mother.

PANEL SIX

Close-up of Ben as he stares at Cass, eyes narrowed.

BEN
The way you talk, though… that’s all Cain.

PANEL SEVEN

Close-up as Cass tilts her head at Ben curiously.

CASS
Knew… my father?

BEN (OFFSCREEN)
In passing.

PANEL EIGHT

Close-up as Ben scowls slightly.

BEN
Even when I was… the way I was… that guy creeped me out. Always talking about raising kids to ‘speak body language’ instead of real words. Train some ultimate fighter.

PANEL NINE

Medium shot as Ben looks pointedly at the scattered, unconscious figures.

BEN
Guess it worked after all, huh?

CASS (OFFSCREEN)
Hm.

PAGE SIX

Nine-panel grid.

PANEL ONE

Medium two-shot. Both Cass and Ben look towards the entrance to the Temple behind them.

BEN
This place was supposed to be empty. Don’t know how they heard it was here already.

CASS
What's here?

PANEL TWO

Medium two-shot. Ben looks at her, surprised, as Cass shakes her head.

BEN
You don’t know?

CASS
No.

BEN
Then why are you here?

PANEL THREE

Close-up of Cass. She’s returning Ben’s stare, and for the first time looks serious, maybe even worried.

CASS
Need… help.

PANEL FOUR

Close-up of Ben, who’s boring holes into her with his eyes.

BEN
What kind of help?

PANEL FIVE

Back to the close-up of Cass.

CASS
My mother... missing.

PANEL SIX

Medium two-shot of Cass and Ben, staring at each other.

BEN
Disappearing is what she does.

CASS
This is… different. I think… in trouble. Think… she… needs help.

PANEL SEVEN

Close-up of Ben, still staring at Cass.

BEN
I know your mother well. If she's in trouble, she can get herself out of it. And if you save her… she'll never forgive you for it.

PANEL EIGHT

Close-up of Cass. She’s glancing off to the side, away from Ben’s gaze.

CASS
We fought. I won. Could have... killed her. But didn't.

PANEL NINE

Close-up as Cass returns her gaze to Ben, staring with an intensity to match his own.

CASS
She already... won't forgive.

PAGE SEVEN

Nine-panel grid.

PANEL ONE

Return of the medium two-shot. Ben has one hand under his chin, watching Cass in silence, evaluating.

PANEL TWO

Same shot as PANEL ONE, except now Ben is speaking.

BEN
I’m sorry, how exactly did you get here, again?

CASS
Climbed.

BEN
Right. Okay.

PANEL THREE

Medium two-shot. Ben’s looking back at the Temple.

BEN
All right. I’ll help you. But I’ve come this far -- I can’t just leave. So lemme make you an offer. You help me, and when we’re done, I help you. Deal?

CASS
… deal.

PANEL FOUR

Medium shot as Ben turns, walking towards the Temple and gesturing for Cass to follow him, which she does.

BEN
What do you know about the Sensei?

CASS
Your Sensei?

BEN
No, that’s O-Sensei. I mean the Sensei . From the League of Assassins.

CASS
Uh…

PANEL FIVE

Close-up on Cass, who’s clearly racking her brain here.

CASS
Assassin Sensei? Old. Ra’s’s father. Or… son? I forget.

BEN (OFFSCREEN)
Never really been sure, myself.

PANEL SIX

Medium shot as Ben kneels down by the Temple door, sliding something into the lock.

BEN
Either way, he used to be the head trainer for the League of Assassins. I got treated to a taste of his medicine while I was there. But he and Ra’s had sort of a… falling out, around the same time Richard and Shiva rescued me. A guy gets the feeling it was related, but I was in a pretty bad place. My memories are spotty, y’feel?

PANEL SEVEN

Close-up as Cass stares into the distance, frowning.

CASS
Shiva… saved you?

BEN
Oh. Yeah.

PANEL EIGHT

Close-up on Ben’s face as he works. He looks pensive.

BEN
Your mother… she was always intense. But she wasn’t always like this. She used to have friends. She used to smile. Hell, she even used to crack bad puns, sometimes.

BEN
I don’t know. I guess the signs were always there. But it’s hard to believe she went from saving me from the League of Assassins…

PANEL NINE

Medium over-the-shoulder shot of Ben as he walks through the now-open door.

BEN
… to training their next generation.

PAGE EIGHT

Seven panels. One wide panel, then two rows of three.

PANEL ONE

Wide panel. Wide interior shot of an ANCIENT TEMPLE. The place is covered in layers upon layers of dust. Ancient statues adorn the hallway, most of them smashed or damaged, as though from a fight.

PANEL TWO

Close-up as Cass looks around at the Temple, slightly awed.

PANEL THREE

Close-up on one of the destroyed statues.

CASS (OFFSCREEN)
Was there… fight here?

BEN (OFFSCREEN)
I’m getting to that.

PANEL FOUR

Close-up as Ben stares ahead determinedly. He looks resolute, and slightly uncomfortable.

BEN
This was the site of my last mission for the League. I was supposed to bring back a map for them.

PANEL FIVE

Close-up of an ANCIENT MAP, laying on a stone surface.

BEN (VO)
See, the Lazarus Pits that Ra’s uses are limited. They recharge eventually, sure, but he still only has so many. And when any one of them gets contaminated, it’s a real danger to him. But Ra’s wasn’t the first one to unlock the Pits. And this map was supposed to have hundreds more suitable locations, all uncharted. Maybe even the source of the whole thing, according to some stories.

PANEL SIX

Medium shot of Ben as BRONZE TIGER, walking through these same halls, which look just as dusty as in panel one, but substantially more intact. The SEPIA tint of the coloring tells us that this is a flashback.

BEN (VO)
I’d been on the trail of it for weeks. Finally heard a rumor that it was here, and came to check it out myself.

CASS (VO)
They… trusted you? Alone?

BEN (VO)
Alone? No.

PANEL SEVEN

Continuing the flashback. Pan back to see Ben’s accompanying LEAGUE HANDLER, a generic assassin.

BEN (VO)
I had a partner for the whole thing.

PAGE NINE

Six-panel grid, entirely in flashback.

PANEL ONE

Medium flashback shot as Bronze Tiger and his partner in what looks to be the temple’s inner sanctum. Bronze Tiger’s partner leans over the MAP, examining it, while Bronze Tiger keeps watch by the door.

BEN (VO)
It’s funny, I can’t even remember her name now. Aida? Asha? Something like that.

PANEL TWO

Similar to the previous shot, except that Bronze Tiger is blocking a flying kick from RICHARD DRAGON.

BEN (VO)
Well, anyway, that was where Richard and Shiva found me. Guess they’d been on my trail the whole time, and they’d finally caught up with me.

PANEL THREE

Medium action shot. Bronze Tiger and Richard Dragon are now locked in intense combat, grappling with each other. Neither Shiva nor Bronze Tiger’s partner are anywhere in sight.

BEN (VO)
I only half remember it, but I can tell you it was a hell of a fight.

PANEL FOUR

Medium action shot. Bronze Tiger and Dragon have taken their fight back out into the rest of the temple, and are smashing the hell out of the place as collateral damage.

BEN (VO)
I didn’t make it real easy on him…

PANEL FIVE

Close-up on Ben’s face as he snaps out of the brainwashing, Richard Dragon pinning him down.

BEN (VO)
… but eventually, Richard got through to me. And we decided that if this place had kept that map from the League all these years… it could keep it from them a while longer. Guess we should’ve just burned it, but… I don’t know. It didn’t feel right, destroying something like that. And we thought that no one else would ever know.

PANEL SIX

Medium shot. Richard helps Ben back to his feet.

BEN (VO)
But I guess they figured it out anyway. That’s why I’m here. To get it before they can.

CASS (VO)
You think… partner… told?

BEN (VO)
Oh. That. No, it’s not that. Thing is… she was fighting Shiva.

Splash page.

PANEL ONE

Flashback splash page. Shiva stabs Bronze Tiger’s partner through the chest.

BEN (VO)
You can put two and two together.

Nine-panel grid.

Close-up. Cass looks down, upset.

CASS
Oh.

PANEL TWO

Close-up. Ben stares determinedly ahead, not looking at Cass.

BEN
Yeah.

PANEL FOUR

Nine-panel grid.

CASS

Medium shot. Ben looks over his shoulder at Cass. He says nothing, apparently inviting her to continue.

PANEL EIGHT

Cass shakes her head.

CASS
Never again. Never.

PANEL NINE

Ben puts his hand on the door to the inner temple, looking back over his shoulder at Cass as he does so. It looks a lot like what we saw in the flashback.

BEN
Yeah? Well, you know something?

Nine-panel grid.

PANEL ONE

Close-up of Cass’s BOOTS as she walks across the floor.

BEN (OFFSCREEN)
I’m --

BEN
-- oh, hell.

Guardian.

Yes.

PAGES SIXTEEN AND SEVENTEEN

The following two pages serve as a mirrored two-page spread. If you're not an artist, just know that this is a cool-ass mirrored setup. Otherwise: the left page is eight panels. Three run vertically up the left 1/3 of the page. The right 2/3 of the page uses the remaining five panels, one as a large panel taking up half the page, and the remaining four as a grid below it. The right page is arranged as a mirror of the left, except that its left 2/3 are arranged as 8 panels (2 wide, 4 high) and the right 1/3 is 3 vertical panels.

8 panels, see above.

PANEL ONE

Medium shot. Ben looks up in alarm as loud thudding noises (SFX: THD, THD, THD) come from offscreen.

BEN
What the–

PANEL TWO

Wide shot of a SENTRY STATUE standing in the doorway. It’s very big, a bit busted up, but still pretty intact looking. Think a stone warrior statue, but ancient enough for some of its features to have worn down over time, and very, very big.

BEN (OFFSCREEN)

I do.

PANEL EIGHT

PANEL EIGHT

BOOM.

PANEL NINE

PANEL TEN

Shot from behind as Cass stands, staring down at the remains of the statue (SFX: hff, hff)

Eight panels.

½ width, Close-up. Ben stares at Cass, disbelieving and exasperated. He’s shaking his head.

And people said I was hot-headed. You’re as bad as your mother, you know that? Trying to punch everything you see.

½ width. Close-up on Cass, who looks stricken.

I can imagine Shiva’s reaction to your little vow, but if O-Sensei could see what Sandra Wu-san’s daughter became… going halfway around the world, fighting statues and teaming up with guys like me, just because she thought her mother was in trouble, well... I think he’d be pretty damn proud.
