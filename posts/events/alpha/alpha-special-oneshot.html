Panel One
Low angle on Detective JOHN JONES, shuffling down a Denver street with his hands in the pockets of beat-up trench coat. He’s wearing a fedora, bent slightly out of shape. It looks like a pretty rough neighborhood. The sun is nearly, but not quite, set behind him. Pedestrians on the street are sparse, but there are a few here and there, including a man in sunglasses and a leather jacket a ways behind John. The color palette should be washed out, almost black-and-white. John has seen a few too many Noir movies and it’s reflected in the visuals.

  1. REED: [Caption] Yo, it’s Reed.
  2. REED: [Cap] There’s been a bit of a… development with the Martian.
